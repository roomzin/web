Story of a Challenge

Complex Booking Search


Imagine a user searching for: 2 single rooms in an economy hotel with WiFi, pool, parking, near specific lat/long, pay-at-property, breakfast included, for 3 nights under $120.

In a typical setup, you'd face complex cache model designs, multiple round-trips and in-memory joins, sync issues with updates under heavy load and expiries. Switch to a DB? Latencies kill high-TPS performance. Need HA? That's heavy resources, effort, and costs to provision infra and maintain order.
